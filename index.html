Why Thailand

Why choose Pattaya
for your business?

Pattaya has evolved into one of Southeast Asia's most dynamic business hubs — combining a strategic location with an exceptional quality of life.

  • ✓

    Strategic Southeast Asia Location

    Direct access to ASEAN markets of 680 million consumers. Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport just 90 minutes away.

  • ✓

    Competitive Business Costs

    Significantly lower operating costs than Singapore, Hong Kong or Bangkok. Office space, staff and living costs at a fraction of Western prices.

  • ✓

    Growing International Community

    Pattaya hosts one of Asia's largest expat communities with strong infrastructure, international schools and world-class healthcare.

  • ✓

    Thai BOI Incentives

    Thailand's Board of Investment offers significant tax incentives and privileges for qualifying foreign businesses.
